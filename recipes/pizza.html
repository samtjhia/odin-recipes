Pizza Recipe

An image of Pizza.

To make a classic pizza at home, start with the dough: mix 2 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon of salt, 1 teaspoon of sugar, and 1 packet (2¼ teaspoons) of instant yeast in a bowl. Gradually add about ¾ cup of warm water and 1 tablespoon of olive oil, mixing until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-10 minutes until smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.

Once the dough has risen, preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). Punch down the dough and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer the rolled dough to a pizza stone or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce over the base, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese on top, and add your favorite toppings such as pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, or olives. Drizzle a little olive oil over the top and season with salt, pepper, and dried oregano or basil.

Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned. Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice it into wedges. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil or a sprinkle of red pepper flakes if desired. Enjoy your homemade pizza with a side salad or garlic bread for a complete meal!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 packet (2¼ teaspoons) instant yeast
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Pizza sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Favorite toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)
  • Olive oil for drizzling
  • Salt, pepper, and dried herbs (oregano, basil)

Steps

  1. Mix flour, salt, sugar, and yeast in a bowl.
  2. Add warm water and olive oil, mixing until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough for 5-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover, and let it rise for 1 hour.
  5. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  6. Punch down the dough and roll it out to your desired thickness.
  7. Transfer the rolled dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  8. Spread pizza sauce over the base, leaving a border.
  9. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  10. Add your favorite toppings and drizzle with olive oil.
  11. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes until golden brown.
  12. Remove from the oven, let cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
